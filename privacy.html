 vbAccelerator's Privacy Pledge
   

vbAccelerator believes that strong electronic privacy is crucial for the ongoing success of the Internet as a service, commercial, and entertainment medium. To this end, unless you designate otherwise, the information you enter here will be known to only two parties: you and vbAccelerator.

We pledge that vbAccelerator will not release your personal data to anyone else without your consent - period.

You may change the status of whether you wish to receive vbAccelerator offers and communications at any time.

When we do present information to our advertisers, this is provided in accordance with our strict privacy policy. For advertisers working directly with vbAccelerator, any statistics provided are in the form of aggregate statistics compiled among all of our visitors' answers to survey questions as well as grouped on-site behavior, unless you tell us otherwise.

vbAccelerator also works with a third party that serves ads to this site. To find out more about how Flycast manages the privacy of information in conjunction with serving ads on this site, please go to http://www.flycast.com/about_us/about-privacy.html.
