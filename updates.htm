vbAccelerator Site Updates

vbAccelerator's Highly Trained Operators set the controls for the next site update (patent applied for, but refused)

The aim at vbAccelerator is to get a site update out at least once every month. Or if not once a month, then at least approximately once a month. And if isn't approximately a month, then you can be certain that it will be within the next month following the month I originally planned to get the update out. Or maybe at the start of the next one. Erm...

Sometimes, I just can't put the stuff out I want to. The code can decide it just isn't going to get working that week (this is always due to limitations in Visual Basic, of course). Other times my inbox becomes overwhelmed by messages (although thanks to the inaccurately named Outlook "Express", this only takes about 4 or 5 messages...).

Occasionally I even have to do some real life work too, such as updating my bookmarks and browsing the Dilbert site.

The upshot of all this is, sometimes you have to wait a bit. Unfortunately I'm not able to answer all the emails I receive either; although I certainly read all of them and I'm definitely very pleased to get your feedback.

On a more useful note, you can now get a Newsletter announcing the updates to the site as they happen, in association with Goffreddo's Visual Basic Source Code Library.
